Info

Want to visit Norway in June?

Of course you do!!!

CSSConf is one event out of three during the first week of June. The other two events are the two day conference Web Rebels and NodeConf Oslo.

Interested in buying tickets for more than one event? Excellent, because all three events are releasing tickets at the same place and at the same time. There will also be combo-tickets available.

The tickets for CSSConf will be priced at 2100 NOK.

Get your ticket here!

Part of the international CSSconf family

Since the prototype, CSSconf US, was started by Nicole Sullivan in 2013, the CSSconf family has expanded around the globe. All CSSconf organizer teams share the vision of an international, diverse and creative CSS community, for which the CSSconf series offers a platform to meet, hack, share knowledge and build friendships.

Venue

Dansens Hus, Norway’s national stage for dance, is located in the Vulkan area of Oslo next to the Akerselva river. It's really close to the Grünerløkka district where you can find a lot of design shops, little cafés, restaurant and bars.
